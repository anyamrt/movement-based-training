# Adding Images to Your Website

## Images Needed

### 1. Hero Background Image
- **File name:** `hero-background.jpg`
- **Recommended size:** 1920x1080px or larger
- **What to use:** Gym environment, you training a client, or action shot
- **Where to place:** `/src/assets/images/hero-background.jpg`

### 2. Profile/About Photo
- **File name:** `james-profile.jpg`
- **Recommended size:** 800x800px (square)
- **What to use:** Professional headshot or training action shot
- **Where to place:** `/src/assets/images/james-profile.jpg`

### 3. Optional Additional Images
- Service icons/photos (if desired)
- Testimonial photos (with client permission)
- Equipment photos
- Training session photos

## How to Download Images from Your Wix Site

### Method 1: Right-Click Save
1. Visit https://www.movementbasedtraining.com.au/
2. Right-click on each image you want to save
3. Select "Save Image As..."
4. Save to a folder on your computer

### Method 2: Browser Developer Tools
1. Visit your Wix website
2. Press F12 (or Cmd+Option+I on Mac) to open Developer Tools
3. Click the "Network" tab
4. Filter by "Img"
5. Refresh the page
6. All images will appear in the Network tab
7. Right-click on image URLs and open in new tab to download

### Method 3: Contact Wix Support
- Login to your Wix account
- Access your media library
- Download all images directly
